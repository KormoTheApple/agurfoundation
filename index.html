Our Mission

Use God’s direction in stewarding His Resources entrusted to Agur Foundation to create facilities where people’s lives can be transformed by the Saving Grace of Jesus Christ and where the fruits of the Spirit are taught and demonstrated.

How We Serve Churches

Facilities

Agur Foundation helps congregations move into permanent facilities -

  • Find a qualified start-up congregation, and match them with a facility which meets their church plan
  • Complete due diligence on the facility and then acquire the facility
  • Fix, remodel, re-fashion, and retool the facility to fit the start-up congregation’s church plan.
  • Finance the facility for 3 years at favorable interest rates with “interest only payments”
  • Pass through Agur the interest payments received to fund international churches and domestic ministries.

Consulting

Agur consultants may help your church through:

  • Site Selection:
  • Site Evaluation (Preliminary Checklist Prior to Purchase of Property)
  • Due Diligence (Refer Consultants to you who can complete Environmental Studies, Survey, Title Evaluation, Preliminary Municipal Discussions)
  • Entitlement of Property (Refer Consultants to you who can complete Land Planning, Civil Engineer, Architects, Traffic Engineers)
  • Horizontal Development (Refer to you Horizontal Development Contractors)
  • Vertical Cost Estimates (Refer to you Commercial Contractors)
